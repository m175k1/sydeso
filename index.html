About Us

Sydeso is a startup company and for us it was a gift from God, I've been praying for this when I was still in abroad to have a business that is related to my interest. It all started when i post on a group in facebook page and I ask if I can join to some group so i'll be able to learn and enhance my skills in programming. There's no group there so i decided to make one and that's where I met my team, Emil and Marvin. I know that its God's way to build our company which is Sydeso. Sydeso is aiming to be one of the best IT services in the Philippines and we also have a mission to build a projects where we can help others. I know the Sydeso did not just happen for nothing but it happened because it has a purpose according to God's plan.
